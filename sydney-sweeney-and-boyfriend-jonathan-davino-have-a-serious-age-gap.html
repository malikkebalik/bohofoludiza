Sydney Sweeney and Boyfriend Jonathan Davino Have a Serious Age Gap

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Euphoria Season 2 wouldn’t be the same without Sydney Sweeney as Cassie. The actor perfectly portrays the role, and her love for Nate Jacobs is both relatable and unhinged. So, what about the actor’s actual love life? Sydney Sweeney’s boyfriend, Jonathan Davino, has been by her side for years — and they reportedly have quite an age gap. Here’s Sydney Sweeney’s age compared to Jonathan Davino’s age.

Who is Sydney Sweeney’s boyfriend, Jonathan Davino?

Sydney Sweeney’s boyfriend in Euphoria Season 2 is Nate Jacobs (played by Jacob Elordi), but the two aren’t dating in real life. So, who is Jonathan Davino, Sweeney’s boyfriend fans rarely hear about?

According to Cosmopolitan, Davino owns restaurants in Chicago, and his family owns the pizza restaurant Pompei. The two have reportedly dated since 2018, marking four years together in 2022. They were first photographed together in October 2018 while in attendance at an InStyle and Kate Spade dinner in Los Angeles. Since that photograph, they’ve been seen in photos together ever since. Though it doesn’t appear that Davino has social media, and Sweeney’s Instagram doesn’t contain photos of her boyfriend, either.

Sweeney spoke to Cosmopolitan about dating. “I don’t date actors or musicians or anyone in entertainment because I can just be normal Syd that way and it’s easiest,” she said, adding that she looks for “a best friend. I need to be able to be with someone who I can literally hang out with 24/7 and never get sick of and we laugh every single day.” 

What is Sydney Sweeney’s age, and how old is Jonathan Davino?

With Sydney Sweeney’s boyfriend reportedly dating her for years, it seems like they have a solid relationship. And they also reportedly have quite an age gap. So, what is Sydney Sweeney’s age, and how old is Jonathan Davino?

Cosmopolitan reports Davino is 37 years old, and Sweeney is 24. They have a 13-year age gap.

Despite their eyebrow-raising age gap, it seems Sweeney learned from past relationships regarding what she wants and deserves. In an interview with C Magazine, she explained she once got caught up in “dangerous relationships” as a result of acting out during her teenage years.

“The acting-out Syd tried to find love through boys,” Sweeney explained. “I got myself into sometimes really unfortunate and even dangerous relationships.”

Also in the interview, she added, “I never talk about it, but I have a very healthy, very steady personal life.”

The ‘Euphoria’ cast member is sparking engagement rumors with a new ring

Sydney Sweeney and boyfriend Jonathan Davino might be ready to tie the knot after several years together. E! News reports a photo captured Sweeney wearing a huge diamond ring on her ring finger. The photographer snapped the photo on Feb. 28, 2022. Sweeney hasn’t said anything about the potential engagement.

Fans excited by the idea of a potential engagement shouldn’t anticipate Sweeney talking about it. The Euphoria actor made it clear she uses her social media for business only.

“I would love to share my normal life so that people can see that it’s not all glamour,” she told Cosmopolitan. “But I can’t because one, I like my privacy, and two, social media is another platform for business. Sharing my life on it could go against the integrity of the business and brand I’m trying to create.”
