Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Xanders Bad Dream Is a Premonition Sarah &amp; Victoria Kidnapping Ah

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers reveal that Xander Cook (Paul Telfer) didn’t rest very well once he was released on bail. After Xander got passionate with Sarah Horton (Linsey Godfrey) and fell asleep with her, he woke up uneasy over a nightmare.

In Days’ March 19 episode, Xander talked about seeing Sarah holding Victoria Horton-Cook as they headed into a tunnel in the distance.

Unfortunately, Xander wasn’t able to get to his family – and once he finally did end up in the tunnel, Sarah and Victoria were gone.

After Sarah assured Xander that it was just a dream, Xander reflected on the reason why he had it.

In Xander’s opinion, it was about the charges that were still hanging over him.

However, that nightmare perhaps seemed more significant than that. What if there was a deeper meaning to that bad dream? Could it have been a premonition?

We don’t have Susan Banks (Stacy Haiduk) around to interpret things for us, but she would probably say that seems like some sort of grim prediction about the future.

If Xander fears he could be trapped behind bars indefinitely, why were Sarah and Victoria heading into that tunnel and getting lost?

It made it seem like Victoria and Sarah were the ones stuck in trouble.

Perhaps Xander has interpreted the dream all wrong and will realize that once a kidnapping crisis happens.

Some shocking news could change Xander’s whole perception!

Days spoilers suggest that Sarah and Victoria could easily get abducted at some point, especially if the walls close in on Konstantin Melelounis (John Kapelos).

If Konstantin’s plot to swindle Maggie Kiriakis (Suzanne Rogers) out of the Kiriakis fortune ultimately fails, could he kidnap Sarah and Victoria to hold them for ransom?

That might be an option to consider, especially if Xander’s nightmare is a clue about what’s to come.

Down the road on Days, maybe that dream could help Xander if he realizes a tunnel is indeed where Sarah and Victoria are being held.

Sarah talked about there being light at the end of that tunnel as she worked to reassure Xander about his nightmare.

That could be the case in reality too if Xander comes to Sarah and Victoria’s rescue!

Days of Our Lives spoilers say Xander, Victoria and Sarah will inevitably have more DOOL drama ahead of them, so stick with us for updates on all the excitement.
