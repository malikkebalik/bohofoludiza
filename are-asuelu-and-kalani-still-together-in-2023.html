Are Asuelu and Kalani Still Together in 2023?

July 2024 · 4 minute read

90 Day Fiancé Season 6 couple, Asuelu Pulaa and Kalani Faagata, have had a rollercoaster relationship. Now, they’re giving their marriage one last shot on 90 Day: The Last Resort. Here’s an update on where they are now and if they’re still married in 2023.

Asuelu and Kalani first appeared on ’90 Day Fiancé’ Season 6

Kalani met Asuelu while on vacation in his native country of Samoa in 2017, as seen on 90 Day Fiancé Season 6. He worked at the resort where she was staying. After their short vacation fling, Kalani discovered she was pregnant with their son Oliver. In January 2018, they began the process for Asuelu’s K-1 visa so he could move to the United States and they could be a family.

Of course, Kalani’s family was skeptical of her relationship with Asuelu. Tensions grew when shortly after Asuelu arrived in America, Kalani learned she was pregnant again and expecting their second baby. Asuelu slowly earned the blessing of Kalani’s parents, Lisa and Low Faagata, and the couple got married in a romantic boat ceremony in California.

The couple was featured on 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After? Season 5 after their second son, Kennedy, was born in May 2019. Kalani struggled to adjust to her new life as a mom of two. Financial troubles, family drama, and communication issues cause this couple to be teetering on the edge of divorce by the season 5 tell-all.

In 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After? Season 6, the couple seemed to be in a better place in their marriage. Asuelu had started working as a rideshare driver, but while their financial problems began to resolve their family’s house, their communication issues remained unresolved. After Asuelu ended up in a car accident, the couple saw this as a wake-up call and vowed to work on their marriage.

Kalani and Asuelu seemed to break up in 2022

Kalani and Asuelu seemed to be in an on-again, off-again relationship throughout 2022, based on their social media updates. They took a family trip to Hawaii in March 2022. But in a June 2022 TikTok, Asuelu described himself as single. By December 2022, he and Kalani appeared to be back together, though neither would comment on the status of their relationship.

In an Instagram Live, Kalani completely dodged questions about her relationship with Asuelu. It appears that Kalani and Asuelu both currently live in California. In February 2023, Asuelu was spotted at the LA Convention Center Travel show representing Samoa by wearing the traditional Somoan cloth, ie faitagas, during a performance (via Reddit).

Kalani and Asuelu joined the cast of ’90 Day: The Last Resort’

Now, Kalani and Asuelu – who have argued over money, her family, and different approaches to parenting – are appearing on 90 Day: The Last Resort. The show, which premiered in August 2023, brings together five couples from past 90 Day seasons for an intense couples retreat. At the end, they’ll have to decide whether to stay together or split up for good. 

While fans will have to keep watching 90 Day: The Last Resort to discover what happens to Kalani and Asuelu, things aren’t looking good for their future. So far, he’s confessed to being unfaithful and given his wife a “hall pass” to kiss another guy. Unsurprisingly, that move has only created more problems in their relationship. 

Further evidence the two have split? Neither has appeared on the other’s social media in recent months. The Sun also reported that Kalani has a new man in her life, and it sounds like the man who was her “hall pass.” 

90 Day: The Last Resort airs Monday at 9 p.m. ET on TLC. Episodes also stream on Max.
