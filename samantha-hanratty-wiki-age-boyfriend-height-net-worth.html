Meet “Yellowjackets” Actor Samantha Hanratty Wiki | Bio

Samantha Hanratty (age: 27; born on September 20, 1995) is a Hollywood actress from Scottsdale, Arizona, who stepped into the acting industry at 10 and earned recognition by working on “Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man’s Chest,” “The Santa Clause 3: The Escape Clause” and “Boogeyman 2.”

The journey for this actor in the entertainment world began with minor roles that quickly led to more significant opportunities. In 2019 Samantha gathered fame with her performance as Sara in the fantasy drama “Timeline.” She was also in season 8 of the widely popular “The Vampire Diaries.”

Recently, Hanratty captured the hearts of audiences worldwide with her outstanding performance in the second season of “Yellowjackets,” which came out on March 26.

In the series, she plays the role of Young Shauna, a high school student who survives a plane crash in the wilderness with her soccer team. The show follows their journey as they struggle to survive and confront their inner demons in a gripping and intense storyline.

How Old is Samantha Hanratty?

On September 20, 1995, Samantha Hanratty (age 27) was born to her proud parents, Brian and Ellen Hanratty, in the vibrant city of Scottsdale, Arizona, in the United States. She is the youngest of four sisters, with Ashley, Danielle, and Kayla.

With a supportive family by her side, Samantha’s journey in the entertainment world began at a young age. Growing up, Samantha attended the prestigious Laurel Springs School, where she honed her skills and developed a passion for acting.

At the tender age of 10, Sammy landed her first role as Adele in the popular TV series “Passions,” directed by Don Phillip Smith. Her natural talent and captivating presence quickly caught the attention of industry of other directors and producers.

In the following year, Samantha appeared in several notable TV productions, including “That’s So Raven,” “MADtv,” “Desperation,” “Cold Case,” and “Hello Sister, Goodbye Life.”

Samantha Hanratty’s Acting Career

A year after entering the entertainment world, this Arizona native debuted in the film world by playing the role of a British girl in Johnny Depp’s blockbuster hit “Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man’s Chest.”

Meanwhile, she landed a vital part in the action-drama series “The Unit” and played that part for three years alongside her older sister Danielle.

Her excellence in acting led her to even more exciting opportunities, including a role as an elf named Glenda in the film “The Santa Clause 3: The Escape Clause.” She also played the lead role of Chrissa in the 2009 film “An American Girl: Chrissa Stands Strong,” showcasing her versatility and range as an actress.

Hanratty’s career peaked after her selection to play the role of Kassidi, Carl’s wife, in the hit Showtime television series “Shameless.” Her compelling portrayal of the character earned her widespread popularity and love from the audience.
