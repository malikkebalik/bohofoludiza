John Deere 4410 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Yanmar 3TNE88 diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 81.2 ci [1.3 L] engine, which delivers 34.6 hp (25.8 kW) power at rated rpm 2600.

John Deere 4410 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Yanmar 1.3L 3-cyl diesel engine, 2,200 lbs [997 kg] Rear lift (at 24″/610mm), power shuttle transmission, 25.2 qts [23.8 L] Oil capacity, and 2,900 lbs [1315 kg] weight.

John Deere 4410 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a mowing deck, front-end loader, and backhoe.
