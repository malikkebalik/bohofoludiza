What is Jeffrey Osborne's Net Worth?

Jeffrey Osborne is an American musician, singer, and songwriter who has a net worth of $6 million. Jeffrey Osborne was born in Providence, Rhode Island in March 1948. He is the lead singer of the R&B and funk band L.T.D. The band formed in 1968 and released their debut studio album Love, Togetherness & Devotion in 1974. L.T.D. also released the album Gittin' Down in 1974 and released the album Love to the World in 1976.

Their album Something to Love was released in 1977 and reached #1 on the US R&B chart. The band had success with their albums Togetherness in 1978, Devotion in 1979, Shine On in 1980, Love Magic in 1981, and For You in 1983. Their singles "Love Ballad", "(Every Time I Turn Around) Back in Love Again", and "Holding On (When Love Is Gone" reached #1 on the US R&B chart. Osborne released his self-titled debut studio album in 1982 which reached #3 on the US R&B chart. He also released the albums Stay With Me Tonight in 1983, Don't Stop in 1984, Emotional in 1986, One Love: One Dream in 1988, Only Human in 1990, Something Warm for Christmas in 1997, Ultimate Collection in 1999, That's for Sure in 2000, Music Is Life in 2003, From the Soul in 2005, and A Time for You in 2013.
