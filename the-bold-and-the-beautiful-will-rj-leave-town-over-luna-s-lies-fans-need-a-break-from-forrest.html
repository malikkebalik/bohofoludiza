The Bold and the Beautiful: Will RJ Leave Town Over Lunas Lies Fans Need a Break from Forrest

August 2024 · 3 minute read

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers reveal that Luna Nozawa’s (Lisa Yamada) deception has created a countdown to heartbreak. Instead of just trusting her instincts and confessing the whole story, Luna chose to listen to Zende Forrester Dominguez (Delon de Metz) and Poppy Nozawa (Romy Park) about hiding the truth.

Zende doesn’t think RJ Forrester (Joshua Hoffman) would understand if he found out Luna took special mints by mistake and hallucinated that Zende was her bedroom partner.

However, Zende seems to be underestimating just how much RJ cares about Luna.

The same goes for Poppy, who’s terrified of Luna losing RJ for good if she spills the beans.

It’s hard to believe RJ would blast Luna since she had no idea the mints were drugs.

RJ would have plenty of blame for Zende and Poppy if he knew what happened, but he’d probably have a ton of compassion for Luna.

Of course, Luna will never know how RJ would’ve reacted if she’d confessed from the start. Now Luna is officially keeping huge secrets from her boyfriend, so this mess with RJ is going to blow up in her face.

The more RJ praises Luna for her honesty, the worse Luna’s guilt will be in the coming episodes. It’ll be interesting to see how long Luna can live with the weight of it all.

Whether Luna confesses or RJ figures out the real story another way, he’ll eventually discover that his girlfriend isn’t the truthful perfect woman he thought she was.

That could really rock RJ to the core, especially since he’s been acting like Luna is the most wonderful girl on the planet.

RJ’s dialogue is basically just nonstop compliments for Luna and repetitive lines about how in love he is.

That’s led many B&B fans to feel like they could use a break from RJ, who’s definitely been hogging airtime lately.

Some viewers feel like it’s become the Luna and RJ show in some ways, so maybe a little separation period could be a good thing!

You can bet RJ will feel betrayed over the news of Luna’s deception.

Since Luna refused to trust RJ with an admission early on, it could shake the foundation of this couple in a big way.

RJ might dump Luna or at least take a step back to reevaluate the lack of trust in their relationship.

Perhaps RJ could catch a flight out of town and get back to globetrotting as an influencer for a while.

It’d be nice to have a little breather from RJ, and it’d give Luna the opportunity to explore some other drama – like the mystery of who her father is or some other family issues.

Naturally, RJ could come back once his soul-searching is complete, but there’s no denying that RJ’s eating up a lot of the show right now. Could you use a break from RJ, even if it’s a temporary one?

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers say RJ has some shockers in his future, so stay tuned for updates on his response to them.
