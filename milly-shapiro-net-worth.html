Milly Shapiro is an American actress. She’s also a singer. She started her career in 2013, playing the title role in the stage production of Matilda the Musical. She played the said role until the following year. After this, she portrayed Sally Brown in the musical You’re a Good Man, Charlie Brown. She later ventured into television, debuting in an episode of the comedy series Splitting Up Together. At the same time, she gained recognition for starring as Charlie Graham in the horror film Hereditary. She then appeared as herself in the TV program The Boulet Brothers’ Dragula. She had a voice role in 2 episodes of JJ Villard’s Fairy Tales.
