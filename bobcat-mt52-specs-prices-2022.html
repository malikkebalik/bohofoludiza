Bobcat MT52 Specs

Bobcat MT52 Specs: Mini-track loaders like the MT52 can substitute manual labor like wheelbarrows and wheel jacks in small spaces.

They are ideal to move materials like dirt, sand, or gravel around for greater efficiency. They come with quick couplers that permit various attachments to be used instead of the bucket that is standard. They are an essential part of any landscaping project that is limited in space.

Bobcat claims that this is the sole mini track loader equipped with an option for a ride-on-platform which can be put in place or removed in a matter of minutes. This means that the device can be used as an incline-on or walk-behind loader.

Bobcat integrated the functions of two control lever directions and speeding one handle with multiple functions for its mini loader MT52. The loader’s tilt and lift functions are controlled with one lever.

The Bob-Tach attachment mounting mechanism is included on the MT52 to facilitate quick changes between work tools. The MT52 can be used with 15 different attachments some of which are similar to the smaller Bobcat lifters.

Bobcat MT52 Compact Track Loader Key Facts

  • Rated operating capacity of 520 lbs. 
  • The machine footprint is 3 ft. wide and 6 ft. long
  • Operating weight of 2,506 lbs.
  • 18.8-hp, liquid-cooled Kubota diesel
  • 12 GPM of hydraulic flow
